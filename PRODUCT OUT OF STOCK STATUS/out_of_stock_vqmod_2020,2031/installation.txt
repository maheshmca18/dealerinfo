==========================================
                 Installation
==========================================

1. Before install this extension you have to install "vQmod" in your shop.

[NOTE : Opencart version - 1.5.4X ] install vQmod 2.3.2 version.


2. Upload the our extension's "vqmod" folder to your root folder. (No file are replaced, Only one new file)
Done.

3. Refresh/Reload Your Opencart Site.
