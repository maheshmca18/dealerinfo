---------------------------------------------------------
          Out Of Stock Status for Product
---------------------------------------------------------

Description :
The extension shows "Out Of Stock" label when the product's status is out of stock or quantity is 0.

Requirements
OpenCart 2

---------------------------------------------------------------
           Installation Steps
---------------------------------------------------------------
1. Login into your administrator dashboard.
2. Extract our extension's zip file.
3. Go to Extensions->Extension installer.
4. Upload the outofstockstatusforproductv2.ocmod.xml file to install the extension.
5. If installed correctly, you should see a success message.
6. Go to Extensions->Modifications. You should see the “Product - Out Of Stock Status” in the
list. Click the “refresh” button at the top right corner.
7.Reload Your opencart site.
